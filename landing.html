Frequently Asked Questions

What is AI Scraping Stack?

It's an AI-powered web intelligence platform that transforms competitor URLs into structured intelligence: screenshots, business analysis, design audit, and generated website code.

Is it free?

Yes, the basic mode is completely free. Jina Reader works without a key, Ollama is a local AI, OpenRouter has free tier models.

How does website generation work?

The system analyzes 3-5 competitor URLs through 5 AI agents and generates a ready-made HTML/React website based on market best practices.

Which AI models are used?

Local: Ollama (llama3.2, mistral, qwen2.5, qwen2.5vl). Cloud: OpenRouter (Claude, GPT-4o, Gemini, Qwen3) — including free models.

How to bypass Cloudflare?

Use the "Stealth" strategy — Scrapling StealthyFetcher with Playwright bypasses Cloudflare Turnstile automatically.
